Data Sources

Shapes, Boundaries and Elevation

GADM

Useful for getting boudaries for countries, and their subdivisions. Subdivision can vary depending on country.

Indian Boundaries

If working on a project in India, use these official boundaries provided by the survey of India. Scroll to Administrative boundaries and you will find boundaries for district level, taluk level, village level etc. Boundaries are free but other datasets are chargeable.

GeoBoundaries

Database of political and Administrative boundaries around the globe. Very easy to download, simplified files are also available.

GAUL

Provided by the Food and Agriculture organisation FAO of the United Nations. It is sourced from various UN agencies and government organisations etc and then combined together. Learn more about it from here

Natural Earth

Useful for making maps, endorsed by North American Cartographic Information Society (NACIS).
